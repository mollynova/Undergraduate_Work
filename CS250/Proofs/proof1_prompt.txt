The following proof will be in four parts.
Please use the correct mathematical vocabulary
and/or diagrams where appropriate.

Consider the relation f over the real numbers,

f: R -> R,  where f(x) = x^3.

1. Prove or disprove: f is a function.
2. Prove or disprove: f is injective (1-1).
3. Prove or disprove: f is surjective (onto).
4. Prove or disprove: f is bijective (both 1-1 and onto).
